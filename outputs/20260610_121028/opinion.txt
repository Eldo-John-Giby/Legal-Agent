=== VERDICT ===
Based on the evidence presented and the arguments made by both sides, I find the accused, Rajesh Verma, guilty of criminal breach of trust and misappropriation of company funds.

=== FINDINGS OF FACT ===
The key findings of fact in this case are:
1. The accused made multiple transfers totaling ₹12,50,000 from the company account to RV Business Solutions between 2024-01-15 and 2024-04-20.
2. The accused had authority to process payments up to ₹5,00,000, but the transfers in question exceeded this limit and lacked proper documentation.
3. The internal audit report found no invoices, work orders, or service agreements for payments made to RV Business Solutions.
4. The registration records show that the accused's spouse is the proprietor of RV Business Solutions, indicating a potential conflict of interest.

=== CONCLUSIONS OF LAW ===
The conclusions of law in this case are:
1. The accused exceeded his authority by making transfers without proper approval and documentation.
2. The accused's actions constitute a breach of trust and misappropriation of company funds.
3. The prosecution has proven beyond reasonable doubt that the accused acted with dishonest intent.

=== ORDER / REMEDY ===
The accused, Rajesh Verma, is hereby ordered to repay the misappropriated amount of ₹12,50,000 to Zenith Logistics Pvt. Ltd. within a period of six months from the date of this judgment.
