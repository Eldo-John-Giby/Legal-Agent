=== VERDICT ===

After carefully considering the evidence and arguments presented by both parties, I find in favor of the Defendant, Jordan Smith.

The Plaintiff, Acme Corp., has failed to demonstrate that the Defendant's breach of contract was the direct cause of the damages claimed. The evidence suggests that the Plaintiff's own actions, including the late changes to the scope of the project and the denial of timely access, contributed to the delays and rework costs.

Furthermore, I find that the Plaintiff's insistence on maintaining the original deadline, despite the scope changes, was an excessive and unreasonable restriction on the Defendant's ability to work. This restriction, combined with the denial of timely access, created an unreasonable burden on the Defendant.

In light of the legal precedents cited, including PRE_CHINTAMAN_RAO and PRE_MANEKA_GANDHI, I conclude that the Plaintiff's actions did not satisfy the tests of fairness, reasonableness, and non-arbitrariness.

Therefore, I dismiss the Plaintiff's claim and find in favor of the Defendant.

=== FINDINGS OF FACT ===

1. The Plaintiff, Acme Corp., made changes to the scope of the project, which created an unreasonable burden on the Defendant.
2. The Defendant, Jordan Smith, requested database access by 2025-01-15 to stay on schedule, but access was not granted until 2025-02-05.
3. The Plaintiff's insistence on maintaining the original deadline, despite the scope changes, was an excessive and unreasonable restriction on the Defendant's ability to work.

=== CONCLUSIONS OF LAW ===

1. The Plaintiff's actions, including the late changes to the scope of the project and the denial of timely access, contributed to the delays and rework costs.
2. The Plaintiff's insistence on maintaining the original deadline, despite the scope changes, was an excessive and unreasonable restriction on the Defendant's ability to work.
3. The Defendant's breach of contract was not the direct cause of the damages claimed, and the Plaintiff has failed to demonstrate that the Defendant's actions were the sole cause of the damages.

=== ORDER / REMEDY ===

The Plaintiff's claim is dismissed, and the Defendant is not liable for the damages claimed.
