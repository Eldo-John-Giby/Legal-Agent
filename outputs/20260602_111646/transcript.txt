### CONCLUSIONS OF LAW

1. The Defendant's failure to deliver the module by the deadline constitutes a breach of contract.

3. The Defendant's breach of contract caused damages to the Plaintiff, but the amount of damages is disputed.

### ORDER/REMEDY
