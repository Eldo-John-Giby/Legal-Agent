The court finds in favor of the plaintiff, Acme Corp, and awards them $50,000 in damages, plus court costs.

=== DAMAGES ===

The court awards the plaintiff $50,000 in damages, consisting of $35,000 in rework costs and $15,000 in delay costs.

=== COURT COSTS ===

The court awards the plaintiff court costs.

=== PREVENTION OR IMPOSSIBILITY ===

The court denies the defendant's claim of prevention or impossibility, as the court finds that the defendant had a responsibility to follow up on their access request and to notify the plaintiff of any potential delays.
