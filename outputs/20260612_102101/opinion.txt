## Closing Statement by Jordan Smith

Thank you, your honor. In conclusion, I believe that the evidence presented in this case demonstrates that I am not liable for the damages claimed by Acme Corp. The changes to the project scope, the delay in granting database access, and Acme Corp.'s own actions all contributed to the delays and damages incurred.

I urge the court to consider the totality of the evidence and to find that I am not in breach of contract. I request that the court dismiss Acme Corp.'s claims and find in my favor.

## Judge's Opinion

After careful consideration of the evidence and arguments presented, I find that both parties have contributed to the delays and damages incurred. However, I also find that Jordan Smith has not breached the contract, as the circumstances that prevented him from delivering the module on time were beyond his control.

## Ruling

I hereby rule in favor of Jordan Smith, dismissing Acme Corp.'s claims. I find that Acme Corp.'s actions, including the changes to the project scope and the delay in granting database access, contributed significantly to the delays and damages incurred.

## Costs

I order that each party shall bear its own costs.

TERMINATE
