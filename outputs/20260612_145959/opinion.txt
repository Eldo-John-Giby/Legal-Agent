=== VERDICT ===
Based on the evidence presented, the Judge finds in favor of the Plaintiff, Acme Corp. The Defendant, Jordan Smith, has failed to deliver the software module by the agreed-upon deadline, resulting in $50,000 in damages.
=== FINDINGS OF FACT ===
The Judge finds that the Defendant was aware of the changes in requirements and had requested additional time and resources to complete the project. However, the Defendant failed to provide sufficient evidence to support their claim of impossibility.
=== CONCLUSIONS OF LAW ===
The Judge concludes that the Defendant's failure to deliver the software module on time constitutes a breach of contract. The Plaintiff is entitled to damages for the losses incurred due to the Defendant's breach.
=== ORDER / REMEDY ===
The Judge orders the Defendant to pay $50,000 in damages to the Plaintiff.
