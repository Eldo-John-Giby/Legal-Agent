=== VERDICT ===

After careful consideration of the evidence and arguments presented by both parties, the court finds in favor of the Plaintiff, Acme Corp. The court concludes that the Defendant, Jordan Smith, breached the contract by failing to deliver the software module on time, resulting in significant damages to the Plaintiff.

The court rejects the Defendant's claims of impossibility and lack of access to necessary resources, finding that the Plaintiff's requests for additional reporting fields and delays in granting access were not the sole cause of the breach. The court also finds that the Defendant's own project management and resource allocation decisions contributed to the delays and the eventual breach of contract.

The court awards the Plaintiff damages in the amount of $50,000, comprising $35,000 in rework costs and additional expenses incurred due to the delays.

3. The Defendant failed to deliver the software module on time, resulting in significant damages to the Plaintiff.
4. The Plaintiff incurred $35,000 in rework costs and additional expenses due to the delays.

=== CONCLUSIONS OF LAW ===

1. The Defendant breached the contract by failing to deliver the software module on time.
2. The Plaintiff is entitled to damages for the breach of contract.
3. The Defendant's claims of impossibility and lack of access to necessary resources are unfounded.
