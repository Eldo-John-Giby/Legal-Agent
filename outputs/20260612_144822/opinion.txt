=== VERDICT ===
The court finds in favor of the plaintiff, Acme Corp. The defendant, Jordan Smith, is liable for breach of contract and must pay damages to the plaintiff.

=== FINDINGS OF FACT ===
The court finds that the defendant failed to deliver the software module by the agreed-upon deadline, resulting in delays and additional costs for the plaintiff. The plaintiff's changes to the scope of work did not excuse the defendant's failure to perform.

=== CONCLUSIONS OF LAW ===
The court concludes that the defendant's failure to deliver the software module by the agreed-upon deadline constitutes a breach of contract. The plaintiff is entitled to damages for the delays and additional costs incurred as a result of the defendant's breach.

=== ORDER / REMEDY ===
The defendant is ordered to pay the plaintiff $50,000 in damages.

=== FULL WRITTEN OPINION ===
The court's decision is based on the evidence presented and the applicable law. The defendant's failure to deliver the software module by the agreed-upon deadline was a clear breach of contract. The plaintiff's changes to the scope of work did not excuse the defendant's failure to perform. The court has considered all the evidence and arguments presented and has made its decision based on the law and the facts of the case.
