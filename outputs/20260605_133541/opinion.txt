=== VERDICT ===
The Plaintiff's claim for damages is dismissed due to the Plaintiff's significant role in causing the delay and resulting damages.

=== FINDINGS OF FACT ===
- The Plaintiff requested changes to the requirements on 2025-01-10 (E2), which impacted the project timeline.
- The Defendant agreed to the revised scope (E5), but the Plaintiff insisted on keeping the original deadline despite the changes and delays in granting access to necessary resources (E4).
- The Defendant's email on 2025-01-12 (E3) clearly stated the need for database access by 2025-01-15 to stay on schedule, but the access was not granted until 2025-02-05 (E4), significantly delaying the project.
- The internal Acme timeline (E7) shows that integration started on 2025-03-01, which is well after the original deadline of 2025-01-31 (E1).

=== CONCLUSIONS OF LAW ===
- The Plaintiff's actions, including the late changes and denial of timely access, were the primary causes of the delay and resulting damages.
- The Defendant's defense of impossibility/prevention is valid due to the Plaintiff's role in causing the delay.

=== ORDER / REMEDY ===
The Plaintiff's claim for $50,000 in damages is dismissed. The Defendant is not liable for the damages incurred by the Plaintiff.

The court has carefully considered the evidence and arguments presented by both parties. Therefore, the Plaintiff's claim for damages is dismissed.
